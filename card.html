Developer

A "DEVELOPER" is a person who develop something.A developer is an individual that builds and create software and applications. He or she writes, debugs and executes the source code of a software application.

 Designer

A DESIGNER is a person who plans the look or workings of something prior to it being made, by preparing drawings or plans.

 Writer

A "WRITER" is a person who has written something or who writes in a particular way. The person engaged in writing books, articles, stories, etc., especially as an occupation or profession; an author or journalist.

 Editor

An "EDITOR" is responsible for reviewing, revising, and correcting written material, such as books, articles, reports, and other forms of written communication.
